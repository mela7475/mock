EMERGENCY RESPONSE CATEGORIES
- CALLS ANSWERED WITHIN 24 HOURS -

Fire: Call 9-1-1 / Call Police Dispatch: (225) 562 - 2200 to Report Criminal Activity

  • Gas Leak

    - Raise up windows in your unit
    - What area is the smell coming from
    - Check stove to make sure all knobs are OFF
    - Leave unit for safety if gas is escaping
    - Call 911

  • Electrical Shorts

    - Check to see if the area is sparking , smoking, or burning
    - Check breaker-box to see if a breaker is on OFF
    - Switch the breaker ON if the switch is on OFF
    - Call 911 or 255-562-2200 if there is a fire

  • Toilets Stop-up

    - Be sure to have a plunger on hand or borrow one
    - Use warm water and bleach to pour down toilet
    - If maintenance is able to come out to un-stop toilets, charges will apply if something is found in the lines

  • Lock-Outs

    - Purchase extra keys for family members

  • Sewer Line

    - If light is blinking call in and staff will contact contractor to see if available to come out

  • Smoke Alarms

    - Always keep extra batteries to prevent constant beeping signal
    - If you see smoke or a fire call 911 or 255-562-2200
